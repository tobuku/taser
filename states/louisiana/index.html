Louisiana Taser Laws — Overview

Louisiana law does not restrict civilian ownership of tasers. No permit is required. Louisiana has a strong castle doctrine and stand-your-ground law.

// La. R.S. § 14:95 — Illegal carrying of weapons

What Is Allowed

  • YPurchase and ownership without a permit
  • YCarrying openly in public
  • YCarrying concealed in public
  • YCarrying in a personal vehicle
  • YCarrying in most private businesses unless posted
  • YUse in lawful self-defense
  • YPurchase by adults 18 and older

Restrictions & Prohibited Locations

Even in permissive states, certain locations prohibit weapons including tasers:

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a taser legal in Louisiana?

Yes. Tasers are legal for civilians in Louisiana with no permit required.

Do I need a permit to buy a taser in Louisiana?

No. Louisiana does not require a permit, license, or registration to purchase or carry a taser.

Where can I buy a taser in Louisiana?

Licensed taser dealers operate throughout Louisiana. You can also purchase civilian TASER models directly from Axon.com or through major retailers including Amazon. Find a local dealer.
