Georgia Taser Laws — Overview

Georgia law does not restrict civilian ownership of electronic control devices. No permit or license is required. Georgia has a stand-your-ground law and no duty to retreat before using force in self-defense.

// O.C.G.A. § 16-11-121 — Definitions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a taser legal in Georgia?

Yes. Tasers are legal for civilians in Georgia with no permit required.

Do I need a permit to buy a taser in Georgia?

No. Georgia does not require a permit, license, or registration to purchase or carry a taser.

Where can I buy a taser in Georgia?

Licensed taser dealers operate throughout Georgia. You can also purchase civilian TASER models directly from Axon.com or through major retailers including Amazon. Find a local dealer.
